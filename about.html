Our Story

Layerlink was founded with a singular vision: to transform the lives of visually impaired individuals through technology. Our journey began with a team of passionate innovators from diverse fields, united by a common goal. Today, we are a leading name in the development of implantable and wearable vision technology, driven by our commitment to excellence and impact.
